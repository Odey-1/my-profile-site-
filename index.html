What Is Coding?

Coding simply put, is the process of writing instructions for a computer to carry out. It's like telling a computer how to think and act.

What Is Coding Used For?

Coding is used for many things such as:

  • Building Websites
  • Mobile apps
  • Games
  • Robotics
  • Artificial Intelligence(AI)
